Rocket League Review

Rocket League is an excellent game

Rocket League is currenlty available on 4 different platforms: Xbox, PlayStation, PC, and Nintendo Switch. The cost of the game is $19.99 CAD on all platforms. Xbox PlayStation PC Nintendo Switch

Casual Game Modes

Rocket League updated their Casual Game Modes playlist on September 24 2018, switching over Rumble, Dropshot, Hoops, and Snowday to the ranked playlists. The only remaining unranked game modes are casual 3s, 2s, 1s, and 4s

Trading

Rocket League has one of the largest platforms for interactive playerbase thanks to its highly popularized trading system. Players can unlock different items in a variety of manners and trade them to obtain their dream car. There are 8 different item rarities:

  1. Common
  2. Uncommon
  3. Rare
  4. Very Rare
  5. Import
  6. Exotic
  7. BLack Market
  8. Limited

Players can unlock items either through crates, tradeups, or trades. Players can win crates through games, but can only open them using a key, which is tradeable on all platforms. Players can unlock anything from rare to black market items in a crate, with a 55% chance of getting a rare item, a 28% chance of getting a very rare item, a 12% chance of getting an import item ,a 4% chance of getting an exotic item, and finally a 1% chance of getting a black market item.
Through tradeups, players can use 5 items of the same rarity from the same collection (type of crate) to obtain an item of the next rarity from the same collection. FInally, players can trade all items from uncommon to limited with eachother.
